E-mailing list

We have started a mailing list for concise announcements regarding meetings, positions, etc. pertaining to game-theoretic statistics, e-values and any-time valid testing. The list is hosted on the list server of the University of Twente (link). It is called e-mail (pun intended). You can subscribe by sending a blank e-mail (no body, no signature, no subject) to e-mail-subscribe-request@lists.utwente.nl. You then will get an e-mail with a link to confirm your subscription. Only subscribers can post to the mailing list: you can just send your messsage to e-mail@lists.utwente.nl. Other features and user manuals can be found under the link above.
