E-Day: November 8, 2024

On November 8 I’m organising a Dutch e-value meet-up: e-Day (feel free to join also if you’re not based in NL). It will be held at CWI (Science Park 123, Amsterdam) from 11.00 till 16.30. It is meant as in informal meet-up for e-value researchers: there will be no tutorials for those new to e-values (but I will organize a winter/summer school on e-values in the next 2 years).

There will be talks, open problem sessions, and time for interaction – the program will be made when most of us have registered and indicated their preferences. Then there will be drinks, and after that, for those who want, we will have dinner together (on your own costs).

Registration is free but mandatory, via this form.
